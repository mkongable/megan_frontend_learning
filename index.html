About Me

Hello! My name is Megan Kongable! I am a recent mechanical engineering graduate from North Dakota who is currently pursuing my master’s degree in plastics engineering and a computer science certificate from University of Massachusetts Lowell! In high school, I attended an introduce a girl to engineering day that opened my eyes to the possibilities that become available with an engineering degree.

I began my journey into engineering in the mechanical engineering discipline focusing on polymers and coatings. I adored my coatings classes and realized that I wanted to dive deeper into plastics engineering, specifically bio-based and biodegradable plastics.

During COVID, I began to teach myself how to code. Starting with Java, I used Hackerrank and Leetcode to learn the syntax and how to put my thoughts into a clear solution. From there, with the help of a friend, I learned OOP design principles and expanded my knowledge of data structures. Currently, I am proficient in Java, Python, and C++.

When I’m not in school or learning to code, I enjoy playing with my pets, watching movies with my friends, and exploring new places. I have two cats: Penelope and Vivienne, and have been known to rescue stray kittens. I’m a huge sci-fi fan and my best friend and I have watched Lost In Space, The Expanse, a bunch of the StarWars series, and both Stargate series! We are currently bingeing the Orville in our free time! I love travelling, especially to places that have a science museum and a zoo, and learning about the history of an area. I enjoy hiking and my photos page on this website has just a few of the photos I’ve taken!

Thank you for checking out my website! Feel free to contact me if you have any questions! I am currently seeking a mechanical or software engineering role in the Austin, TX area or remotely!
